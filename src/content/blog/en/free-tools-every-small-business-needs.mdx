Running a small business means doing the work and the paperwork. The paperwork does not have to cost anything. Below are the free tools we think every small business, freelancer, and trader should have bookmarked. Each one does a single job well, works in any currency, supports configurable tax rates, and gets you a clean, downloadable document in under a minute.

## 1. A free invoice generator

Invoicing is the one task you cannot skip, because it is how you get paid. A [free invoice generator](/tools/free-invoice-generator) lets you create a professional, properly structured invoice with your branding, line items, multi-currency totals, and tax, then download or send it instantly. No spreadsheet formulas, no formatting headaches.

## 2. A quotation generator

Before the invoice comes the pitch. A clean quote wins trust and sets expectations. Use a [free quotation generator](/tools/free-quotation-generator) to send a polished estimate, and when the client says yes, convert it straight into an invoice rather than retyping everything.

## 3. A proforma invoice generator

When a customer needs to pay a deposit, clear customs, or get internal approval before you deliver, a proforma is the right document. A [free proforma invoice generator](/tools/free-proforma-invoice-generator) gives you that preliminary bill in the correct format, clearly marked as not a tax invoice.

## 4. A receipt generator

Once you are paid, send proof. A [free receipt generator](/tools/free-receipt-generator) produces a clear payment receipt that records the amount, method, and date, which keeps customers happy and your records tidy.

## 5. A purchase order generator

Buying as well as selling? A [free purchase order generator](/tools/free-purchase-order-generator) lets you issue clean POs to your suppliers so both sides agree on quantities and prices before anything ships.

## 6. A delivery note generator

When goods move, a delivery note travels with them. A [free delivery note generator](/tools/free-delivery-note-generator) documents exactly what was sent and received, which heads off disputes over short or wrong deliveries.

## 7. A VAT calculator

Tax math is small but easy to get wrong, especially when discounts are involved. A dedicated [VAT calculator](/tools/oman-vat-calculator) works out the net, tax, and gross at the rate you set. In the GCC that is usually 5%, but you can apply any rate for other countries.

## Why use free tools that connect

Standalone tools are useful, but the real time savings come when they work together. Every generator above is part of [free invoicing software](/free-invoicing-software) that is free forever for sales and invoicing. That means your quotes flow into invoices, invoices flow into receipts, and everything stays in one place with consistent numbering, currencies, and tax. When you are ready for inventory, HR, accounting, or reporting, you can add paid modules without switching platforms.
