Overview

One platform for your whole company

Paper & Pen is a multi-tenant cloud ERP built in Oman for GCC small and mid-sized businesses. It connects every part of your operation, sales, customers, stock, production, people, money and the bank, on a single secure platform. Start with the modules you need today and switch on the rest as you grow. Everything is bilingual Arabic and English (plus Hindi, Bangla and Urdu), priced and built for the region, and you can be live in minutes.

What sets it apart

AI & automation, built in

📥 Magic Inbox
Email or upload a supplier invoice; AI extracts it, matches the vendor and bank payment, and drafts the bill for one-click posting.
🤖 Anna AI assistant
An always-on assistant connected to email and WhatsApp, with AI business insights across the system.
🪪 Face-recognition kiosk
Web-based biometric attendance with photo capture, no separate hardware or native app required.
🏦 Bank automation
Live statement sync with smart auto-reconciliation, including split-payment matching, plus secured transfers.
